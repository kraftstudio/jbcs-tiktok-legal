Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how the JB Construction Services marketing assistant application operates.

The application is used internally by JB Construction Services Pty Ltd to research TikTok trends, hashtags, and content ideas and to generate marketing captions for construction marketing.

The application does not collect, store, or process personal data from TikTok users.

The application only accesses publicly available TikTok content and analytics.

No user data is shared with third parties.
